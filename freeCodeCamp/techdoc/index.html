Nutrition

What you eat defines how you feel, how you perform, and how you thrive. Eating a balanced diet is key to achieving optimal health.

Focus on whole foods, and ensure a proper balance of macronutrients like proteins, fats, and carbohydrates.

  • Prioritize fruits and vegetables

Exercise

Exercise is more than just a way to stay in shape — it’s a way to keep your heart healthy, boost your energy, and maintain mental well-being.

Engage in a combination of strength training, cardio, and flexibility exercises for a well-rounded routine.

const workout = "30 min cardio + 15 min strength";

Schedule time for physical activity daily, even if it's just a short walk. Your body will thank you.

  • Aim for at least 150 minutes of moderate activity weekly
  • Include rest days to recover
  • Focus on consistency, not perfection
Mental Health

Mental health is an essential part of overall wellness. Prioritizing self-care and managing stress is just as important as physical health.

Practice mindfulness, meditation, or any activity that helps calm your mind and refresh your outlook.

const selfCare = "Take breaks + Talk it out";

Don’t hesitate to seek help or talk to someone if you feel overwhelmed. It’s a sign of strength, not weakness.

  • Practice gratitude daily
  • Maintain social connections
  • Set boundaries for work-life balance
Sleep

Sleep is the ultimate recovery tool for both your body and mind. Quality sleep supports memory, mood, and even immune function.

Aim for 7-9 hours of sleep every night to let your body recharge and heal.

let sleepHours = 8;

Develop a bedtime routine that signals to your brain it’s time to wind down.

  • Avoid screens before bed
  • Stick to a consistent sleep schedule
  • Create a relaxing environment
Hydration

Water is life. Staying hydrated keeps your organs functioning properly, helps with digestion, and even boosts your mood and energy levels.

Make sure to drink enough water throughout the day, especially if you’re active or exposed to heat.

Track your water intake and set reminders to drink more, especially if you forget.

  • Carry a reusable water bottle
  • Infuse water with fruits for flavor
  • Avoid sugary drinks
